## 🎮 Level 0: The Trinity Persona Strategy
To build a resilient "FloodPulse," the system must serve three distinct archetypes. Each persona validates a specific technical layer of our Google Cloud / Gemma stack.

**1. The Stranded Commuter**

**Identity:** Sarah, a professional working in Upper Hill who lives in South B. She is currently caught in a flash flood at the T-Mall underpass.

**User Story:** "As a Stranded Commuter, I need a one-touch SOS Pulse that works even when my data plan is exhausted, so that my location is added to the city's Cloud Spanner Graph for rescue coordination."

**Technical Validation:** Event-Driven SOS (Level 2) and high-stress UI/UX.

**2. The Local/Boda-Responder**

**Identity:** Juma, a 28-year-old high-mobility courier in Nairobi with deep knowledge of the Mbagathi "back-alleys."

**User Story:** "As a Boda-Responder, I need to use the Gemma 4 31B vision model to 'pulse' the road ahead during a storm, so I can safely navigate to stranded users without getting my bike stuck in alluvial mud."

**Technical Validation:** On-device multimodal inference and offline navigation.


**3. The Urban Planner/Strategist**

**Identity:** Kamau, a lead coordinator at the Nairobi Urban Planning department monitoring the Mbagathi basin breach.

**User Story:** "As an Urban Strategist, I need to use Graph Orchestration to see where commuters are concentrated and where responders are available, so I can run Consensus Protocols to prevent bridge overcrowding."

**Technical Validation:** Cloud Spanner Graph GQL queries and Multi-Agent Orchestration (Level 3 & 4).
